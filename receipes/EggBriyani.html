Egg Biriyani

Description

Egg biryani recipe is a splendid coming together of fragrant long-grain rice and spiced and saut√©ed boiled eggs. Basmati rice is cooked in whole spices, layered with some biryani masala and eggs, and topped with fried onions, herbs, and saffron milk. The pot is then sealed and put on gentle heat to make a deliciously spicy egg dum biryani.

the spices frying in hot ghee and the fresh mint and cilantro leaves are going to fill your kitchen with some heavenly aromas. Don't be surprised if your neighbours turn up at your doorstep to find out what's cooking:)

Ingredients

  • Ghee
  • Ground spices - red chilli, biryani masala, cumin
  • Eggs
  • ggs
  • Fried onions
  • Salt, ground paprika and black pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Mint and cilantro leaves
  • Whole spices - black cardamom, green cardamom, bay leaves, black peppercorns, cloves
  • White Basmati rice

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs - I like using my instant pot to hard boil the eggs because peeling eggs boiled in the instant pot is a breeze.
  2. Make the ginger garlic paste - homemade paste tastes so much better.
  3. Fry the onions
  4. Chop the tomatoes, mint and cilantro leaves.
  5. Wash and soak the rice
  6. Fry the eggs
  7. Make the curry masala
  8. Cook the rice
  9. Assemble the biryani
